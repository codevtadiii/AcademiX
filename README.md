# AcademiX
ABSTRACT OF THE APPLICATION :
The “AcademiX” is an innovative platform designed to help university students connect with peers for academic support, collaboration, and mentorship. Students often face challenges finding the right collaborators for projects, study groups, or even resolving subject-related doubts. This application leverages machine learning to match students based on skills, interests, and project needs, ensuring productive and effective partnerships. It bridges the gap between students from different sections and academic years, fostering a culture of collaboration and mentorship. Users can seek guidance, share knowledge, and collaborate on academic and extracurricular projects. The platform not only helps resolve doubts and promotes skill-sharing but also encourages juniors and seniors to interact, creating opportunities for mentorship and deeper connections. By enabling such interactions, the application enhances learning outcomes, builds a supportive academic community, and prepares students for real-world teamwork. The result is an inclusive and engaging environment where students can grow academically and personally while supporting one another.
